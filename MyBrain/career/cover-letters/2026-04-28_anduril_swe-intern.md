Dear Anduril Hiring Team,

I'm applying for the Software Engineering Intern position (Summer 2026) at Anduril. I'm a Junior at UC San Diego pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering with a focus in Machine Learning and Controls, and I'm a U.S. Citizen — eligible for export-controlled work without sponsorship.

What I find compelling about Anduril is that you're shipping autonomous systems where the cost of "not working" is real. In ECE 148 last quarter (Winter 2026), I worked through the full hands-on autonomous vehicle stack on a Raspberry Pi: configuring an OAK-D Lite stereo camera, integrating a VESC motor controller, training a CNN behavior-cloning model in TensorFlow, then deploying it for autonomous driving. I also went through GPS-based path following and a ROS 2 integration lab. None of these were research-grade systems, but going through every layer (Linux permissions, sensor drivers, ML pipeline, ROS 2 messaging) gave me a real sense of how brittle the boundary between "works in simulation" and "actually moved" can be — and how much engineering discipline lives in that gap. That's exactly the kind of work I want to spend a summer on.

For the team's recycling-collection final project, I owned the Python servo-control script for the shovel mechanism, while the rest of the four-person team handled YOLOv8 detection, ROS 2 state machine, and 3D-printed mechanics. I learned a lot from working across disciplines (ECE / CompE / Aerospace / Mechanical) — Anduril's mission scale would be an order of magnitude bigger version of that.

I'd be excited to contribute to Lattice OS, autonomy, or sensor fusion teams — wherever an undergrad intern can add value. Thank you for considering my application.

Sincerely,
Javen Jiawen Cao

## 📋 Cover Letter 设计说明（不进 PDF）

### 整体策略
- **~280 词**（业内 sweet spot 250-350）
- **不背 ATS keyword**——cover letter 是给真人看的，不需 keyword stuffing
- **不演不装**——开头不背"我从小就梦想"那种 cliche
- **真诚 + specific**——用 ECE 148 的 hands-on 细节作为 anchor

### 4 段结构对应
1. **开头**：申请岗位 + 一句身份 + **U.S. Citizen 强调**（Anduril 强制要求）
2. **为什么是我（technical fit）**："working through the full stack" + Linux/sensor/ROS——直接 match Anduril 主线
3. **为什么是 Anduril**："cost of not working is real" + "engineering discipline in that gap"——强调 mission criticality 而非 vague fanboyism
4. **结尾**：诚实承认 undergrad intern 定位（"wherever an undergrad intern can add value"）

### 关键 framing 决定
- **没装"我热爱国防"**——这种不真心的话面试会被识破。改用"shipping autonomous systems where the cost of 'not working' is real"——抽象但真实
- **诚实写 final project 是 team-of-4 + 你 own 的部分**——和简历一致
- **不提 Lattice OS 名字超过 1 次**——recruiter 看到狂吹品牌反而 cringe
- **结尾不夸大"perfect fit"**——只说"add value"，姿态正确

### 备选改动
- 如果想**更短**（~200 词）：删第 3 段中"None of these were research-grade systems..."那段长句
- 如果想**强调 ML 而非 robotics**：把第 2 段 "autonomous vehicle stack" 换成 "ML pipeline + edge deployment"
- 如果想**加 recruiter hook**：第 1 段最后加 "I would be glad to walk through my ECE 148 work in more detail at any time"
